Bitter Parent

This app uses the Nutritionix API to provide your child with a less-sugary alternative to their favourite junk food, and uses Firebase to store pairs of compared foods. Or hey, give it a whirl if you're looking to cut out sugar from your everyday diet. It was built using mob programming, with Jessica Chin and Mohamed Omar.
